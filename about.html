About This Guide

Why “Knowing Cuba” exists

This guide was created to help travelers explore Cuba through simple, friendly, and accessible information. Instead of overwhelming you with hundreds of options, it focuses on key places, helpful tips, and visual stories that give you a realistic first impression of the island.

It is designed for first-time visitors, students, and anyone curious about Cuba’s culture, beyond stereotypes. The goal is to share the island’s beauty and complexity in a way that feels welcoming and easy to follow.

I wanted to share the real beauty of Cuba and offer a helpful starting point for anyone planning a trip — or anyone who simply wants to learn more about Cuban history, food, music, and everyday life.

How content is created

This site combines personal explanations with carefully sourced information, images, and videos.

What I create myself

Short descriptions of Cuban history, explanations of foods and traditions, simple “fun facts,” and summaries of music and cultural elements — all written in a clear, friendly tone.

What I source legally

Images from Pexels, Unsplash, and Pixabay. Videos embedded from YouTube or Vimeo when embedding is allowed. Text is paraphrased and cited from sources like Wikipedia, Britannica, and official Cuban tourism websites.
